History of Favergamez

FaverGamez began as a small Newcastle toy store more than fifty(50) years ago, serving generations of families with classic board games, action figures, and puzzles. Over the decades, it became a trusted name for fun, creativity, and togetherness in the community.

ith the gaming industry evolving rapidly, the board of directors envisioned a new future. Inspired by the global excitement for the Nintendo Switch 2 and its record-breaking 3.5 million pre-orders within just four days, FaverGamez decided to invest in modern gaming experiences. This transformation marks a bold step from traditional toys to cutting-edge video games, blending the company's heritage with the future of entertainment.
